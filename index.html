About me

- Probably overthinking

Hi, I’m Shoaib Akhtar, a Kolkata-based Digital Marketer, self-taught Frontend Web Developer, and Graphic Designer. I started my journey during the lockdown in the year 2020 — not from a tech background, but as a commerce graduate driven purely by passion.

Without any formal training, I learned everything on my own — from coding and design to SEO and running social media ads. I’ve worked with a few small businesses so far, and while I don’t have a long client list yet, I make sure to give 100% to the ones I work with. Helping them grow online with real, measurable results is what keeps me going.

I also operate under the name GrowWithShoby — a solo startup with a mission to provide honest, affordable digital solutions to individuals and businesses looking to go online the right way.
